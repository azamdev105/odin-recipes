One Bowl Chocolate Cake

Let's Make it

One Bowl Chocolate Cake has a deep, rich chocolate flavor with a moist, tender crumb. Its taste is enhanced by high-quality cocoa powder and sometimes a hint of coffee, which amplifies the chocolate notes without a coffee taste.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

    • Steps

    1. Step 1

      2. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.

    2. Step 2

      3. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.

    3. Step 3

      4. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.

    4. Step 4

      5. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.

    5. Step 5

      6. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

    6. Step 6

      7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

    7. Step 7

      8. Enjoy!
